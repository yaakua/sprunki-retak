## What is Sprunked 2.0?

Sprunked 2.0 is an exciting and challenging platformer game where you control a bouncing ball through various obstacles and levels. With improved graphics, smoother controls, and new challenging levels.

This sequel brings even more fun and excitement than the original Sprunked game.
